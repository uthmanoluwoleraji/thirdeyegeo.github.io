Representative Experience of the Principal Consultant

Major Transport Infrastructure – Victoria

Geotechnical site investigations and groundwater assessments for freeway and rail upgrades including drilling supervision, groundwater monitoring installation, seepage assessment, construction and design support.

Dewatering and Risk Assessment – Mining, Residential & Commercial Development

Hydrogeological assessment to support open pit dewatering schemes assisting mine planning decisions. Environmental and hydrogeological assessment to support evaluation of groundwater dewatering at proposed construction sites.

Contaminated Land & Groundwater Modelling

Application of analytical transport solutions and groundwater flow assessment to understand contaminant plume behaviour and monitoring requirements.

Water Resource & Environmental Projects

Hydrogeological impact assessment studies and modelling supporting water resource management, dam and spring protection under Australian environmental regulatory frameworks.

About Third Eye Geoscience

Third Eye Geoscience & Subsurface Consulting is led by Uthman who holds a Bachelor of Science (Hons) degree in Geophysics, Diploma in Laboratory Techniques, a Masters in Petroleum Geoscience and a Postgraduate Certificate in Groundwater Hydrology. His experience cuts across technical involvement in the Niger Delta and UK North Sea upstream oil and gas sector, and Australian infrastructure, mining and environmental projects. He is a member of the Australian Institute of Geoscientists. The practice focuses on integrating geological, geotechnical and hydrogeological data into clear, decision-ready advice.
