About Third Eye Geoscience

Third Eye Geoscience & Subsurface Consulting is led by Uthman who holds a Bachelor of Science (Hons) degree in Geophysics, Diploma in Laboratory Techniques, a Masters in Petroleum Geoscience and a Postgraduate Certificate in Groundwater Hydrology.

His experience cuts across technical involvement in the Niger Delta and UK North Sea upstream oil and gas sector, and Australian infrastructure, mining and environmental projects. He is a member of the Australian Institute of Geoscientists.

The practice focuses on integrating geological, geotechnical and hydrogeological data into clear, decision-ready advice that supports engineering design, regulatory approval and risk management.

Core strengths:
• Translating complex subsurface behaviour into clear reporting
• Integrating geological, geotechnical & hydrogeological data
• Working collaboratively with designers, contractors and regulators to de-risk subsurface uncertainty.
